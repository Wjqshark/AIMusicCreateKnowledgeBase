# 📝 歌词模版：标准流行结构（含 Pre-Chorus）(Pop Standard)

## 适用风格
Dance-Pop, Pop, K-Pop, C-Pop 主流

## 结构说明

```
[Intro]       × 0–2行（可选，器乐或人声）
[Verse 1]     × 6–8行 — 场景铺垫
[Pre-Chorus]  × 4行  — 情绪上升，悬念感
[Chorus]      × 4–6行 — 全曲最强 Hook
[Verse 2]     × 6–8行 — 深化故事
[Pre-Chorus]  × 4行
[Chorus]      × 4–6行
[Bridge]      × 4行  — 转折
[Chorus]      × 4–6行（可升调）
[Outro]       × 2行
```

## 英文模版示例（夏日爱恋主题）

```
[Verse 1]
You were standing at the corner in the sun
White t-shirt and a smile that got me done
I pretended that I didn't even see
But my heartbeat was louder than the beat

[Pre-Chorus]
Feels like the whole summer stopped in that one breath
Like nothing else around us even left

[Chorus]
You're the rain I've been hiding in my chest
All the words I never said out loud
Even if the whole world's moving fast
I wanna stay right here with you somehow

[Bridge]
Maybe I should just say it to your face
Maybe love is just a leap of faith
In every silent moment that I had
I kept choosing you

[Outro]
I keep choosing you...
```

---

## 使用说明

1. **Pre-Chorus 关键**：末句应停在属和弦（V）或不完整句，制造进入副歌的紧迫感
2. **副歌第一句**是全曲最重要的句子，应是最抓耳的 Hook
3. Bridge 提供情感转折，通常去掉鼓点或改变编曲织体
