# 📝 歌词模版：说唱结构 (Rap / Hip-Hop Verse)

## 适用风格
Hip-Hop, Trap, Drill, 中文说唱

## 结构说明

```
[Hook]    × 4–8行 — 旋律型副歌，抓耳，重复
[Verse 1] × 16行  — 说唱，叙事/炫技
[Hook]    × 4–8行
[Verse 2] × 16行
[Hook]    × 4–8行
[Bridge 或 Verse 3] × 8–16行（可选）
[Hook]    × 4–8行
[Outro]
```

---

## 说唱歌词写作规则

### 押韵模式
```
AABB: 每两行押韵（最常见）
ABAB: 交替押韵（更有节奏感）
多音节尾韵: 同时押多个音节（高级技巧）
内韵 (Internal Rhyme): 句中也押韵
```

### Flow 类型说明
| Flow 类型 | 特点 | 适用风格 |
|---|---|---|
| Straight | 每拍都踩，正拍为主 | Old School |
| Syncopated | 跨越拍点，切分感 | Trap, Boom Bap |
| Double-time | 说话速度是 BPM 两倍 | Fast Rap, Freestyle |
| Triplet | 三连音，"Migos flow" | Trap |

直到那些声音变成了回应

我不问别人给不给机会

## 英文模版示例（Trap 风格）

```
[Hook]
Started with nothing, now I'm rising up
Every single night I had to fill my cup
They said I wouldn't make it, look at me now
Told myself I would and I'll be damned if I bow

[Verse 1]
I remember when the rent was overdue
When a dollar had to stretch the whole week through
Couldn't sleep, mind racing, ceiling staring back
Now I'm building something they can't hold me back

Nothing handed to me, everything I earned
Every lesson in the struggle something that I learned
Wrote my name in the dark, no one saw me then
Tell me who's laughing now, say it once again
(continue 8 more bars...)
```

## 使用说明

1. 每个 Verse 建议 16 bars（行），每行对应歌曲中的 2 拍或 1 小节
2. Hook 应极度抓耳，可以是旋律型（唱）或节奏型（说）
3. 说唱歌词不强制押尾韵，但要有律动感（读出来有节奏）
4. 中文说唱注意音调（声调）与 Beat 的配合
