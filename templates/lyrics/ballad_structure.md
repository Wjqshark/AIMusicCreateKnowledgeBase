# 📝 歌词模版：标准流行情歌结构 (Ballad Structure)

## 适用风格
Pop Ballad, R&B Ballad, 华语情歌, Indie Pop

## 结构说明

```
[Verse 1] × 8行 — 场景铺垫，第一人称叙事
[Chorus]  × 4–6行 — 情感核心，反复出现
[Verse 2] × 8行 — 故事深化，补充细节
[Chorus]  × 4–6行
[Bridge]  × 4行 — 情绪转折，可升调或反转视角
[Chorus]  × 4–6行（加 Ad-libs 或升调）
[Outro]   × 2–4行（可选，循环副歌淡出）
```

## 英文模版示例（失恋主题）

```
[Verse 1]
Your jacket's still hanging by the door
The coffee you left is cold on the floor
My phone lights up but it's never your name
Guess I'm the only one playing this game

[Chorus]
I thought that love could fill up the space
But all I found was an empty embrace
You walked away and I watched you go
And I've been counting seasons, letting go

[Verse 2]
You sent a text, "Hey, how have you been?"
Like throwing a stone in a lake so thin
I typed ten words then I hit delete
Just answered "fine" from a shaking heartbeat

[Bridge]
Maybe you were just passing through
Maybe this is what we were always meant to do
But I still want to ask you one more time
Would you come back if you knew what you left behind?

[Chorus + Outro]
I thought that love could fill up the space...
(fading)
```

## 使用说明

1. LLM 根据用户提供的**主题/故事**，将模版中的占位内容替换为具体歌词
2. 保持每行大致相同的音节数（中文：7–10字；英文：8–12音节）
3. **副歌每次出现内容基本相同**（可在最后一遍加一两句 Ad-lib）
4. Bridge 只出现一次，提供新视角
