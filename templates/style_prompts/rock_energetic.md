# StylePrompt 模版：摇滚激昂 (Rock Energetic)

## 适用场景
- 情绪：激昂、力量、热血、叛逆
- 场景：运动、游戏、励志、对抗
- 人声：男声为主（可女声）
- 平台：Suno v3/v4、Udio

---

## 基础模版

```
rock, energetic, electric guitar, powerful drums, [BPM] BPM,
male vocals, anthemic chorus, distorted guitar, driving bass
```

---

## 变体模版

### Hard Rock 力量
```
hard rock, powerful, heavy drums, distorted electric guitar,
male vocals, aggressive, anthemic, 145 BPM, AC/DC inspired,
arena rock feel, guitar riff driven
```

### Alternative Rock 激情
```
alternative rock, energetic, indie feel, male vocals, 
guitar-driven, raw production, 130 BPM, angsty, intense,
Foo Fighters inspired, catchy chorus
```

### Punk Rock 短促爆发
```
punk rock, fast, short, aggressive, lo-fi, raw, 160 BPM,
simple chords, male vocals, rebellious, high energy, rough edges
```

### 励志 Rock Anthem
```
rock anthem, uplifting, powerful, stadium, male vocals,
electric guitar, sing-along chorus, 128 BPM, triumphant,
Bon Jovi inspired, fist-pumping energy
```
